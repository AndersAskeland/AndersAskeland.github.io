About us

The Danish Society for Extracellular Vesicles (DSEV) is a Danish community of extracellular vesicles-, exosomes-, and microvesicle researchers. The objective of DSEV is to promote, protect, advance and communicate interest and research regarding extracellular vesicles and to promote the interests of Danish scientists nationally and internationally.

As an organization, DSEV connects researchers at its Annual Meeting and reach out to global EV researchers and workshops/events.
